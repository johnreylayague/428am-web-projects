Minglanilla Highlands

Minglanilla Highlands Tubod, Minglanilla Photos Location Updates Reserve now Where the rhythm of life softens and every day feels like a quiet retreat – welcome to Minglanilla Highlands.

Crafted by SterlingLand Residences and Development, Inc. as its crown jewel and flagship project, this extraordinary community rises above the rest, perched on the most picturesque vantage point in Tubod, Minglanilla. From here, you’re treated to sweeping views of the sea and the sparkling cityscape – a daily reminder that life can be both serene and spectacular.

Every detail has been thoughtfully designed to harmonize nature, modernity, and the aspirations of discerning Filipino homeowners. The homes feature sleek, international designs that speak to both elegance and practicality; perfect for those who seek beauty without compromise.

Set in a First Income Class Municipality, Minglanilla Highlands is more than just a residential community. It is Cebu’s first fully integrated, world-class township – a vibrant, sustainable haven built for thriving professionals, growing Christian families, nurturing homemakers, and retirees seeking a rewarding life in their very own sanctuary.

Here, the pace is slower, the days more intentional, and beauty is built into every detail – right outside your door.
