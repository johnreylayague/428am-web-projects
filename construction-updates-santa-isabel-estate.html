Santa Isabel Estate Carcar

Santa Isabel Estate – Carcar marks SterlingLand’s entry into mass housing development with its fresh, forward-thinking concept called “Eco-Value Living.” “Eco” reflects both economic accessibility and ecological mindfulness through the introduction of innovative construction methodology that aims to reduce carbon footprint; “Value” highlights the community’s people-first, holistic features; and “Living” represents a meaningful, vibrant life in a nurturing environment.

The development reflects an affordable residential community designed for hardworking Filipinos. The development blends green architecture with a sustainable lifestyle, by integrating nature into everyday living through pocket gardens, landscaping with native plants, and eco-conscious utilities. Built as a peaceful, secure Christian community, Santa Isabel Estate promotes not just homeownership, but a new kind of lifestyle where they will discover more to life than just living.
